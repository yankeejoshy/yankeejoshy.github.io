Recipe #1: Tomato Pasta

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Trader Joe's Pasta Sauce   8 ounces
Rigatoni   16 ounces
Salt & Pepper   For tasting
Garlic   4 cloves

Instructions

Step 1: Fill a pot of water (deep enough to cover the pasta), add some salt, and bring to a boil. Meanwhile, peel and finely slice the garlic cloves.

Step 2: Once the water is boiling, add the pasta into the boiling water and cover. Stir once in a while, until the rigatoni is slightly soft. Meanwhile, cook the garlic in a pan for half a minute, before adding in the pasta sauce.

Step 3: Drain the boiling water from the pasta pot, and soak it in cold water. Once the pasta is cold, drain the cold water.

Step 4: Add the drained pasta to the pasta sauce. Mix well to coat the pasta.

Recipe #3: Jasmine White Rice

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
White Rice Grains   1.5 cups
Water   1.5 cups
Salt   2 pinches

Instructions

Step 1: Boil the water in a pot. Add some salt for flavor. Meanwhile, wash the rice by soaking it in water, running your hand through it several times, and draining the water from the rice. Repeat this at least twice so that your cooked rice will be less starch-y!

Step 2: Add the rice grains, drained, to the boiling water. Set the heat to medium-low, and cover the pot. Leave the rice to simmer for 15 minutes.

Step 3: After 10 minutes, switch off the heat and leave the rice to be steamed for 10 more minutes. LEAVE the lid on!! Try not to raise the lid to ensure that most of the steam stays in the pot.

Step 4: After 10 minutes, remove the lid and fluff the rice with a large spoon. Serve immediately, or leave it for a few minutes in the open if you prefer drier, chewier rice.
